\subsection{Final Extrusion Presets Results\label{sec:results:extrudingBaSO4:extrusionPresets}}
For all sample mixtures (0\% to 10\% barium sulfate), the initial PLA presets did not have to be adjusted to output good-quality filament. Each sample mixture was extruded at 4RPM.
